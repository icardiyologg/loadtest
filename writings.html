Orchestrating Tools Instead of Replacing Them

The biggest mistake companies make with AI adoption is trying to replace their existing tools. Teams have spent years learning ServiceNow, Jira, or Xurrent—and that knowledge is valuable. Instead of replacement, the smarter approach is orchestration: connecting these systems with AI that sits on top, making workflows smarter without disrupting what already works.
