I believe the best AI solutions start with understanding the business, not the technology. Before writing a single prompt or designing an agent, I ask: who owns this process? Where does the data live? What does "better" actually mean for this team? The tech is exciting, but the goal is always fewer clicks, less noise, and happier operators.
